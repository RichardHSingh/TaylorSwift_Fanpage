Primary School Education


While living her childhood days in a family farm in Montgomery County, Pennslyvania, Taylor Swift was attending West Reading Elementary Centre.
At nine years old, the family then moved to Wyomissing where she had tranferred over to Wyomissing Area Junior/Senior High School.

High School Education


At the age of 14 years old, Taylor's father applied to be transferred to Nashville office in Tennessee along with the rest of the family. This was to aid Taylor Swift break into the music industry specifically country music.
Taylor has initially attended Hendersonville High School then transferred to Aaron Academy after two years. This allowed her to bloom as Taylor was touring the nation and being homeschooled too. Taylor passed high school with a GPA of 4.0.
